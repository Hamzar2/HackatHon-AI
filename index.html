How to Use HealthSphere

Step 1: Install MetaMask

Download the MetaMask extension or mobile app and create a wallet.

Step 2: Fund Your Wallet

Add cryptocurrency to your wallet using an exchange or transfer from another wallet.

Step 3: Deploy Your Smart Contract

Use an online tool like Remix IDE to write and deploy your smart contract.

Step 4: Get the Contract Address

Copy the contract address after deployment and use it to interact with your application.
